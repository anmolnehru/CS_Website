CourseWork

Following are the courses I did/am doing:

    Fall 2006

  • 764 Topics in Database Management Systems

    • I took this course with Prof. Jeff Naughton. Highly recommended considering UW is a great school for doing research on databases and that Jeff is an amazing teacher. Of algorithmic interest: R-trees (Efficient data structures for spatial searching).

  • 707 Mobile and Wireless Networking

    • With Prof. Suman Banerjee. A useful course for any networking grad student. State of the art in wireless research is a different beast when compared to wired networking. Useful project experience on cool wireless stuff is a plus.

    Spring 2006

    /

  • 740 Advanced Computer Networks

    • Necessary course for Qual preparation in Networking.

  • 736 Advanced Operating Systems

    • With Prof. Mike Swift. Highly recommended for Systems and Networking students. Its a pleasure to do a course with Mike. Dave Plonka and I worked on the Buffer Cache paper in this class which won the best paper at USENIX LISA'07. Expect to get good project experience and read excellent papers in the field.

    Fall 2007

  • 787 Advanced Algorithms and Data Structures

    • With Prof. Shuchi Chawla. Useful course for a wide coverage of Algorithms. Recommended.

    Spring 2008 (Ongoing)

  • 838 Topics in Computing

    • Building Fast Networks: With Prof. Cristian Estan

  • 769 Advanced Natural Language Processing

    • With Prof. Jerry Zhu.

  • 642 Introduction to Information Security

    • With Prof. Somesh Jha.
