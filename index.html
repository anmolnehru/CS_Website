Anmol Mohanty

I am a 2nd year Grad student in Computer Sciences at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. I expect to graduate with a Master's degree in CS in May 2016.

I am broadly interested in High performance systems, Databases and Computer Vision. I work with Prof. Vikas Singh and Hyunwoo Kim in applying computer vision and statistical modelling to solve interesting problems with brain images. My main thrust here is to move a complex image processing application developed by Hyunwoo to the Amazon AWS cloud in an economical way without compromising performance. I received my Bachelor's (with Honours) in Electronics and Communication Engineering. from the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur (IIT Kharagpur) in 2013. I also earned a minor in Computer Science from there.
